# Profile
Michael Kirchhof is a Ph.D. candidate in the International Max-Planck Research School for Intelligent Systems (IMPRS-IS), co-supervised by Prof. Enkelejda Kasneci and Prof. Zeynep Akata at the University of TÃ¼bingen. He received his B. Sc. (2018) and M. Sc. (2021) in Statistics with distinction at TU Dortmund University where he focussed on probabilistic modeling and machine learning. In 2019, he was a research intern at BMW Group, Munich. His research goal is to introduce explanations to safety-critical classification tasks.
